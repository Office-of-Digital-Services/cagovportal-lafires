Learn how to replace records from California. Replacements are free for records lost in the fires. You might have to pay a notary fee.

If the birth happened in another state, learn how to get records from other states.

Apply online

To apply online, you’ll need to verify your identity with either a Login.gov account or by signing a sworn statement in front of a notary.

Use Login.gov

Login.gov is a secure, federally backed service for identity verification that provides access to many U.S. government websites with one login.

  • Sign in or create and account with Login.gov
  • Once verified, you can complete and submit your application online in minutes.
  • You do not need to use a notary
Get started with

Use a notary

Select your application below to get started. After filling it out, you need to:

  1. Print the form
  2. Sign it in front of a notary
  3. Upload the signed application to submit online
  • Birth record (English)
  • Registros de nacimiento (Español)

Apply by mail or email

  • Print the application for the record you need.
    • PDF versions are available from the California Department of Public Health.
  • Fill it out, sign it in front of a notary and mail it according to the instructions.

Apply in person

Visit a Disaster Recovery Center. Or go to the county recorder’s office in the county where the birth, marriage, or death happened.
